Fulcrum Travel Japan September 19-29, 2017

Posted by Dan Mori

Fulcrum Travel Japan “Dan Style”

Every once in a while, an opportunity arises to leave the world you know behind to see life through another’s eyes. Our guided surf trip to the island nation of Japan is one of those occasions.

Led by Fulcrum founder, Dan Mori, this adventure to the land of the rising sun will be leaving in September of 2017 and will not be offered again.

If you have any questions or concerns, please don’t hesitate to contact us. We believe whole-heartedly that this will be the most memorable surf trip you will ever take and would love nothing more, than to have you come aboard.

Keep reading to learn about our offering.

The Guide

Dan Mori is the perfect person to lead a surfing excursion through Japan. Not only is he fluent in Japanese, he has surfed and traveled through Japan for the last 20 years. Coupling his world class coaching abilities with fun, beginner friendly waves, this trip will not only improve one’s surfing abilities but also prove to be an enriching cultural experience.

Due to language barriers, most travelers experience Japan in a typical tour-bus setting. Cool if you’re into generic experiences, but if you’re a traveler seeking authenticity, you want to hang with locals and learn how they live. The Waves

Beginning our journey in Tokyo, the first stop for surf is at Shonan, a sand bottom beach break that has playful and clean waves perfect for progressing one’s abilities.

The waters warm and the views from the line up stunning, boasting views of Mount Fuji in the distance. Shonan is home to a bustling and colorful surf scene, but crowds are not competitive like they can be in California. Instead, the Japanese surfers are encouraging and love sharing their stoke with you.

Food and Drink

The Japanese are diligent and innovative chefs who prepare traditional and modern flavors with tremendous attention to detail. If you’re fond of sushi and ramen, your palette will thank you for taking this trip. Because of Dan’s extensive connections in Japan, Fulcrum Travelers will have the opportunity to dine on local delicacies.

After a long day of surfing, there’s nothing quite like an ice-cold beer. Japan has some of the tastiest varieties. There’s a something for everyone when it comes to Japanese nightlife including clubs, bars, and even vending machines that disperse beer. It is legal to drink on the streets in Japan and while this trip is centrally focused on surfing, we’ll be sure to enjoy our time to the fullest extent.
